    A little about me!

    Hi there! My name's Max and I'm a programmer mainly focused on creating Artificial Intelligence for games to help immerse and challenge players. I also do a lot of Gameplay programming. Using these skills I aim to create the most engaging experiences possible.

    I'm currently a student in my final year at Falmouth University studying Computing for Games(BSC). I mainly work with Unity but also have experience with Unreal. I've worked with a variety of languages such as C#, C++, Java, Javascript, SQL, HTML and CSS.

    I'm a keen student and a hard-worker, constantly looking to learn new techniques and expand my technical knowledge through practical and theoretical learning.

Group Projects

Fey (Current Project)

Fey is a 2.5D Metroidvania game using Unity 2018, where you play as a young girl breaking free from her cage and her spirit companion who helps her to escape.

On this project I was mainly responsible for creating the AI that the player has to fight as they traverse through the levels. I also programmed the Combat, Save System and Cutscenes in the game, as well as managing the teams use of Version Control.

Penguin Planet - GlobalGameJam2019

Penguin Planet is a light-hearted resource management game where you have to send your rather well-dressed penguins off to collect resources needed for the colony.
